Jo Jenkins

Jo Jenkins is a conceptual artist from Californias Central Valley, currently based in Irvine, California. With over a decade of passion for photography and digital arts, she earned her Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of California, Irvine in 2025. As a Black woman artist, Jo has witnessed the lack of authentic cultural representation in photography and uses her work to address this gap. Her creative and portrait photography focuses on capturing melanated skin tones with accuracy and subtle vibrance. Photography has also become a deeply personal form of expression for Jo. With losing multiple family members to cancer, she uses her camera as a way to process grief and preserve the memories of loved ones. Jo plans to pursue an MFA degree to further develop her artistic practice and voice.
